Refund Policy

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases.

Eligibility:
- Items must be returned within 30 days of purchase
- Items must be in original condition with tags attached
- Proof of purchase is required

Process:
1. Contact our support team to initiate a return
2. You will receive a return shipping label via email
3. Ship the item back using the provided label
4. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after receipt

Exceptions:
- Sale items are final sale and cannot be returned
- Gift cards are non-refundable
- Personalized items cannot be returned
